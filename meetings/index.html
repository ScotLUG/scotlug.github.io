People gather and meet in the Electron Club (CCA, 350 Sauchiehall Street, Glasgow, G2 3JD) at around 19:00, and talks start at 19:15. The Electron Club is on the first floor of the CCA. We generally migrate to Henglers Circus on Sauchiehall Street after the meeting.
